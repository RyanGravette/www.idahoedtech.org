Sponsorship Levels

Vendor Booth Selection: Starting January 07, 2015 IETA will begin allowing vendors that have chosen a level that allows booth selection to begin selecting booth space. If you register for a booth after that date IETA will do the best it can to accommodate your selection for your vendor level. Please note vendors coming in at a lower level may have already selected their booth space by that date. To make sure you get the full benefit of your sponsorship level please register prior to January 07, 2015.

Silver Level - $1,000 IETA Conference Exhibit Space - one 8’ table, one 10’x8’ booth (booth placement at board discretion), IETA Website Recognition with link to Your Site, Acknowledgment on appropriate conference marketing materials, Recognition on a Vendor Sponsor Level Board, Name badge identifying you as a Silver Level Sponsor.

Gold Level - $2,500 Everything included in the Silver Level, Distribution of Product Advertisement within Conference Registration Packet, Quarter Page ad in electronic booklet, Preferred placement in vendor booth layout (after Diamond, Platinum, and Executive Levels), Name badge identifying you as a Gold Level Sponsor.

Platinum Level - $5,000 Everything included in the Silver, and Gold Levels, Additional Booth space (up to two 8’ tables, two 10’x8’ contiguous booth spaces upon request), Preferred placement in vendor booth layout (after Diamond and Executive Level), Access at IETA regional meetings for one 30 minute presentation at vendor request to be arranged with regional representative, One time promotional email to all conference attendees at vendor request, Half Page ad in electronic booklet, Name badge identifying you as a Platinum Level Sponsor.

Diamond Level - $7,500 Everything included in the Silver, Gold, and Platinum Levels, Additional Booth space (up to three 8’ tables, three 10’x8’ contiguous booth spaces upon request), Five seats for Tuesday awards lunch, Preferred placement in vendor booth layout, One 90 minute breakout session (upon request), Full page ad in electronic booklet, Name badge identifying you as a Diamond Level Sponsor.

Titanium Level (Limit 2) - $15,000 – 1 still available. Everything included in the Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond plus: Ten seats at Tuesday awards lunch; logo on conference backpack; three 90 minute breakout sessions (upon request)Name badge identifying you as a Titanium Level Sponsor.

Lodging

IETA has worked with three area hotels to provide special rates for vendors and attendees. Whether you select one of the hotels below or another hotel of your own choosing, IETA requests that you fill out a hotel information card at the IETA registration desk. Filling out this card helps to offset the cost of the IETA conference as the number of hotel rooms utilized in the area for the conference affects the price that IETA pays for the conference facilities. Your help with this is very much appreciated.

Please be aware that we have a limited number of rooms held each night. The Grove Hotel will only hold our room block until January 12th. If you wish to stay at The Grove Hotel utilizing the IETA rate, you must make your reservation before that date.
